Why the blog?

I've spent my career studying geology and physics and have a passion for understanding the cause of natural phenomena. I have lots of experience looking at large data sets - seismic, gravity, magnetics, electromagnetics, and miscellaneous subsurface geologic data. Most of these projects have been done in MATLAB and ArcGIS.

More recently I've started honing data science skills to bring my intuitions about data exploration and data analysis into the world of machine learning and replicable data science using python frameworks and git. Until now, I haven't had a clear project or structured place to put my mini-projects. Now, this blog can serve as that space! Whether it's simple visualizations with geologic data or building neural networks to predict March Madness brackets - I will aim to document it here!

Where in the world?!

  • Past Experiences at Hess
